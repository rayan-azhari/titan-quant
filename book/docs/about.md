# About & licence

## Author

**Dr. Rayan Azhari** is the author and architect of the systematic trading system
this book documents. The book distils the engineering and methodology behind
taking a trading idea from research to a live, risk-managed, operated process,
the parts that decide whether an edge survives contact with a real market.

Source, framework, and issues: [github.com/rayan-azhari/titan-quant](https://github.com/rayan-azhari/titan-quant).

## What this book is

A practitioner's guide to building a production quant trading stack: validation,
portfolio construction, risk management, deployment, and operations, using a real
system (referred to throughout as "Titan") as the running, sanitised case study.
It deliberately shares *process and methodology*, not proprietary edge. Start at
the [preface](index.md), or jump to [A backtest you can trust](part2-research/backtest-you-can-trust.md).
